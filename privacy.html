Bilal — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Bilal ("the app") is an Adhan, prayer-times, Athkar and Qibla application. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app accesses and how it is used.

Information the app uses

  • Approximate / precise location: used on your device to calculate accurate prayer times and the Qibla direction for where you are. Your coordinates are sent only to the prayer-times and map providers listed below to return times and a place name. They are not stored on our servers (we do not operate any server) and are not used for advertising or tracking.
  • Notifications: the app schedules local notifications on your device to alert you at each prayer time. These are generated locally.
  • Local settings: your chosen language, calculation method and Adhan-sound preference are stored locally on your device only.

Third-party services

  • Aladhan Prayer Times API — receives your coordinates to return prayer times.
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — receives your coordinates to return a city/country name.

Please review their respective privacy policies. We have no control over data handled by these third parties.

Data we collect

None. Bilal has no account system, no analytics, and no advertising SDKs. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Permissions

  • ACCESS_COARSE/FINE_LOCATION — prayer times & Qibla.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — prayer-time alerts.

You can revoke any permission at any time in your device settings; the app will fall back to a default location (Makkah) for prayer times.

Contact
